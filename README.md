# Strategic-Risk-KPI-Analysis-for-a-Specialty-Coffee-Business

## Project Overview

This project presents a strategic and risk management analysis developed for a specialty coffee business in Mexico. The objective was to identify key operational, talent, and customer experience risks, define performance indicators, and propose data-driven strategies to improve organizational sustainability and service quality.

The project integrates business context analysis, risk management frameworks, KPIs, and customer experience mapping to simulate a real-world consulting engagement.

---

##  Objectives
- Identify operational and human capital risks
- Analyze talent management as a strategic asset
- Define key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Apply PMI risk management standards
- Propose actionable mitigation and growth strategies

---

##  Methodology
- Business and industry analysis
- Qualitative and quantitative risk assessment
- Risk identification and classification (threats and opportunities)
- Application of PMI Risk Management Practice Standard
- KPI definition and monitoring strategy
- Customer experience analysis using a Journey Map (Mystery Shopper)

---

##  Analysis & Tools
- Risk matrices and probability-impact analysis
- KPI design for operational, talent, and service performance
- Customer journey mapping
- Strategic recommendations based on risk prioritization
- Business storytelling and executive-level insights

---

## Key Insights
- Talent retention and workplace culture are critical risk factors affecting service quality
- Service perception and employee engagement are strongly connected
- Inventory, supplier selection, and operational scalability represent key operational risks
- Digital presence and product innovation present growth opportunities

---

##  Strategic Recommendations
- Strengthen training and onboarding programs
- Implement incentive and gamification systems for employees
- Improve internal processes to reduce service variability
- Use KPIs to monitor talent, service, and operational risks
- Leverage digital channels to support sustainable growth
